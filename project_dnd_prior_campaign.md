The family (Scott + wife + kids) previously played **Dragons of Stormwreck Isle** — the 2022 WoTC Starter Set adventure. Level 1-3. Setting: small island with a monastery, young bronze dragon **Runara**, cultists led by her villainous half-sister.

## Why this matters

- **Shared mental model.** The family is not brand-new to D&D — they've done one full short campaign. Teaching-ramp scope in PROJECT_KNOWLEDGE.MD (`teaching_mode: heavy/moderate/light/off`) can lean closer to "moderate" rather than full handholding.
- **Continuity candidates.** Surviving characters could carry forward. Runara could return as a mentor NPC. The monastery could be a recurring home base. Island geography + draconic cults as setting motifs.
- **Natural next step.** After a Delian Tomb warm-up (1 session, level 1), Stormwreck Isle's level 1-3 arc fits the planned 3-session intro-campaign slot in PROJECT_KNOWLEDGE.MD's campaign structure. They already know the mechanics; you could compress orientation and focus on narrative + the new hybrid workflow.

**How to apply:**
- When planning the first "real" campaign (post-Delian Tomb test), Stormwreck Isle is a strong candidate because the family will already recognize the setting and characters can be revived.
- Runara is a good reference NPC voice to practice Claude consistency on — the family already has an expectation of her personality from the prior run.
- Avoid retelling the same plot — use setting familiarity as a shortcut, but the actual events should be new.
